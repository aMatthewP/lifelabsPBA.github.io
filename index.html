About us:

We are group 3 from apec schools and we aim to create a fund raising event for the residents of Sitio Bebe. The residents of Sitio Bebe are currently undergoing a health crisis where they have a shortage of health workers and health supplies. It is our goal to create a fund-raising event to give Sitio bebe medical supplies, hire health workers, and create a health center.

Our Content and target audience

For our content we will be focusing on educational videos and news-oriented videos so our audience can be educated about what is going on in Sitio Bebe and what our plans to help them are. We plan to target our content to compasionate humanitarians who will see the situation of Sitio Bebe, philantropists who will see our plans and goals to help Sitio Bebe, and student's and teenagers who can see that they can make a difference by attending our fundraising event.

Our content will consist of educational videos about the situation in Sitio Bebe, which will include the common diseases in the area, the livelihood of the residents, and possible solutions to their situation. Choosing educational videos will make our audience engaged in learning about Sitio Bebe and what we can do about their situation. We will also have news-based videos that inform people about the current events in Sitio Bebe. Choosing news-based videos will inform our audience more about Sitio Bebe's current situation. As people put on news while doing something, our audience can learn about the events happening in Sitio Bebe.

Needs

For the categories that we picked, we would need to know how to speak in front of people and be able to choose words wisely. We also need information about Sitio Bebe and what the health crisis that they are currently facing, possible solutions to their problem, what we are planning to do in order to help them, and information about the fundraising event. Our group chose informative and news-based videos because the general audience would be curious as to what is being shown and they would be exposed to the community of Sitio Bebe. This type of content would show everyone about the situation of Sitio Bebe. It would also show the community mainly the place and the people living in it which would allow people to empathize with said people.
